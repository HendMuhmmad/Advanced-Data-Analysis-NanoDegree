Introduction¶

  • Do movies with higher budgets have higher revenus or ratings?
  • What is the most popular genre for each of the last five years?
  • What is the production company that achieved the most revenue in 2015 ?

    Conclusions¶

    From the budget/revenue plot and according to the given and shown data only we notice that :¶
    • for many cases movies with higher budgets have higher revenue

    • there is no relation between the budget of these movies and vote average
